About Brandon

I am a full-time Biochemistry PhD student at the university of Toronto. Under the supervision of Dr. Grant W. Brown, I am studying how the eukaryotic proteins respond to DNA replication stress, a hallmark of cancer cells. Understanding how cells respond to DNA replication stress will provide greater understanding of cancer biology and guide cancer treatments.
